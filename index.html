A few of my projects

A text-based productivity app

Designed for people who prefer to keep their to-dos in a plain text file, Todoing adds a layer of intelligence and orgaization to your list. Hashtags allow you to filter list items, and a priority matrix lets you categorize items by urgency and time needed to complete.

The order in which you keep your list is important, so the use of the tools never mutates your list, and helpful line numbers are provided throughout.

An app for project managers to resource their teams

Designed specifically for mangers who need to delegate hours to their teams on a weekly basis, Designer Resourcing allows you to build a schedule-like report by assigning hours to the people on your teams within the context of a specific client. View all assignments at once to identify and correct conflicts, or rebalance labor across your team, and say goodbye to emailing out files like schedule-new-revised-updated-1.xls.
